1. Information We Collect

PetroNotes is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Providing access to educational materials
  • Website administration and maintenance
  • Improving user experience

3. File Uploads and Downloads

All uploaded notes are stored securely and are accessible to all users. We do not:

  • Modify the content of uploaded files
  • Claim ownership of uploaded materials
  • Sell or distribute uploaded files commercially
  • Store personal information in uploaded files

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and data stored on our site.

5. Third-Party Services

PetroNotes does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All data is handled exclusively by our own systems.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Admin session management
  • Basic site functionality
  • User preference storage

7. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

  • Ensuring they have the right to upload materials
  • Not uploading copyrighted materials without permission
  • Keeping admin credentials secure
  • Using materials for educational purposes only

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.
